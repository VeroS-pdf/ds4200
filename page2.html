Continuation of the Family

My sister surprised the family by adopting a kitten in early September. She's in medical school, so it was more of an "emotional support" cat. The first kitten to join the family was Pluto! And she was truly intending on only adopting one kitten. However, after doing research online and learning about "only kitten syndrome", I got a direct message from my sister the next day with a picture of Juno. 'I'm just fostering this one' she said. And then she named the kitten Juno. And then she made a shared photo album called "Pluto and Juno Song". And so literally no one was surprised when she ended up adopting Juno as well. So now there are three cats in the family!
